Gate 1: Discovery (10 min)
Your goal is to get a second meeting.

What to Prepare (5-7 min):

  • Problem: What pain are you solving? Who feels it?
  • Solution: What do you do? How does it work?
  • Traction: What proof do you have? (customers, revenue, pilots)
  • Transaction: What are you proposing to THIS buyer specifically?

Gate 2: Qualification (20 min)
Your goal is to prove your business model.
Entry Cost: You must PAY $2,000 to enter this meeting. (See How to Win)

What to Prepare (8-10 min):

  • Traction Deep Dive: Who are your customers? What is your revenue? Show metrics.
  • Business Model: Unit economics (CAC, LTV), pricing, path to profitability.
  • Proof of Scale: Your GTM strategy, sales pipeline, and partnerships.
  • Transaction Specifics: The exact proposal, terms, and timeline.
